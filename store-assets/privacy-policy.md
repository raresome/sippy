# Privacy Policy for SIPPY

**App:** SIPPY (`com.genartstudios.sippy`)
**Developer:** Gen Art Studios
**Last updated:** 10 July 2026
**Contact:** support@genartstudios.com

Gen Art Studios ("we", "us") built SIPPY as a free game supported by ads and optional
in‑app purchases. This policy explains what data the app collects, why, and the choices you
have. We keep it short and plain because there isn't much to explain — SIPPY is a single‑player
arcade game with no accounts and no sign‑up.

## The short version

- SIPPY has **no login and asks for no personal information** — no name, email, phone number, or location.
- Your game progress (best score, unlocked hats, upgrades, settings) is **stored only on your device** and is deleted when you uninstall.
- We show ads through **Google AdMob**, which uses your device's **Advertising ID**.
- We measure how the game is played with **Google Firebase Analytics** (anonymous, aggregated events).
- If you buy something, the purchase is handled by **Google Play Billing**.
- We do **not sell your data**, and all data is sent over encrypted connections.

## Information we collect

We do not ask you for personal information, and SIPPY has no user accounts. The only data that
leaves your device is collected automatically by the third‑party services below.

### 1. Advertising data (Google AdMob)
To show ads, the Google Mobile Ads SDK accesses your device's **Advertising ID** (a resettable
identifier) and limited device/ad‑interaction information. This is used to serve and measure ads
and, where you consent, to personalize them. In the EEA, UK, and Switzerland we present a consent
choice on first launch; if you decline or consent is unavailable, ads are shown in a
**non‑personalized** form. AdMob data is processed by Google as an independent controller under
[Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) and the
[How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites) page.

### 2. Analytics data (Google Firebase Analytics)
We record **anonymous, aggregated gameplay events** — for example when a run ends, when you get
splatted, how much you banked, and when a hat is equipped — so we can understand what's fun and
fix what isn't. These events are tied to an app‑instance identifier, not to your identity, and we
do not use them to contact or track you across other apps. Firebase Analytics is provided by Google.

### 3. Purchase data (Google Play Billing)
If you make an in‑app purchase (e.g. Remove Ads, a hat pack), **Google Play** processes the
transaction and shares purchase status with the app so it can unlock the content. We never see or
store your payment card details. Purchases are non‑consumable and can be restored on a new device
through Google Play.

### 4. Data stored only on your device
Your best score, closest call, banked "blood", owned hats and trails, equipped cosmetics, upgrade
levels, sound setting, and consent choice are saved locally using Android's app storage. This data
**stays on your device**, is not uploaded to us, and is removed when you uninstall the app.

## What we do NOT collect

- No name, email address, phone number, or account.
- No location data.
- No contacts, photos, files, microphone, or camera access.
- No health, financial, or biometric data.

## How the data is used

| Data | Purpose | Shared with |
|------|---------|-------------|
| Advertising ID | Show and measure ads | Google / ad partners |
| Gameplay analytics events | Improve the game (aggregated) | Google (Firebase), not sold |
| Purchase status | Unlock and restore purchases | Google Play |
| On‑device game progress | Save your progress | No one — stays on device |

We do **not** sell your personal information, and we do not use it for any purpose beyond those
listed above.

## Your choices

- **Ad personalization:** In the EEA/UK/Switzerland you can change your consent at any time from
  **Settings → Privacy options** inside the game.
- **Reset your Advertising ID or opt out of personalization:** Android **Settings → Privacy → Ads**.
- **Delete your data:** Uninstalling SIPPY removes all locally stored game data from your device.
  To request deletion of analytics data associated with your app instance, contact us at the email
  above. Advertising and analytics data held by Google is retained per Google's own retention
  policies and Firebase defaults.

## Children

SIPPY is directed to a general audience aged 13 and older and is **not directed at children**. We
do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided
us with personal information, contact us and we will remove it.

## Data security

All data transmitted by the third‑party SDKs above is sent over **encrypted (HTTPS/TLS)**
connections. On‑device game data is stored within the app's private storage sandbox.

## Changes to this policy

If we change how the app handles data, we will update this page and the "Last updated" date above,
and — where required — surface the change in the app.
