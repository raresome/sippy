SIPPY is a push-your-luck arcade game about greed, timing, and one very hungry mosquito. Land on a sleeping giant, hold to drink… and let go the instant before the slap. Every extra drop is worth more — and wakes the giant faster. How much do you dare to bank before the hand comes down?

One more sip. That's how it always starts. That's how it always ends.

🦟 HOLD TO DRINK, RELEASE TO LIVE
The whole game lives in one thumb. Press to sip, and your payout climbs on a greed curve while the giant's irritation creeps up. Let go too early and you leave blood on the table. Let go too late… SPLAT.

😴 GIANTS WITH A TEMPER
Deep Sleepers snore like freight trains and give you room to work. Light Sleepers wake at one wrong slurp. Chaotic Nappers twitch, spike, and lie to you. Read the face, the snore, and the danger vignette — the game screams "let go" without a single word of tutorial.

⚡ CLUTCH THE WINDUP FOR BIG MONEY
When the hand rears back, you enter the windup — the most dangerous, most profitable moment in the game. Escape mid-windup for a CLUTCH multiplier and stack combos across giants. Frame-perfect getaways with 0.05 seconds to spare are the stuff of leaderboard legend.

🩸 SPEND YOUR BLOOD ON UPGRADES
Banked blood buys real power: Thicker Straw (drink faster), Stealth Wings (calmer giants), Ninja Reflexes (more reaction time in the windup), and Blood Magnet (bank more on every escape). Push deeper, greedier, longer.

🎩 DRESS UP THE DOOM
Deck out Sippy with procedurally drawn hats — beanie, halo, party hat, propeller cap, tiny sombrero, top hat, crown, even tiny crocs. Equip a particle trail. And yes — the ghost keeps the hat.

👻 SECOND WIND
Splatted on a big run? Sippy's ghost refuses to leave. Take a second wind, keep your banked blood, and get right back to the greed.

🏆 STATS, ACHIEVEMENTS & LEADERBOARDS
Chase a better night, your closest call ever, and seven achievements from First Blood to Frame Perfect. Compare your best night and tightest escape on Google Play leaderboards.

Perfect for:
• Quick one-more-run sessions in a waiting room or on the bus
• Fans of push-your-luck and high-score arcade games
• Anyone who loves a tense "should I stay or should I go" gamble
• Reflex and timing junkies hunting frame-perfect escapes
• Players who want a funny, shareable game with zero setup

Why you'll keep coming back:
• Dead-simple one-thumb controls, deep risk-reward mastery
• Plays fully offline — no internet needed to have a night
• No sign-up, no account, no nonsense — just tap and go
• Cute, cartoonish art and a comedy of very poor decisions

A quick, honest note: SIPPY is a comedy game with stylized, cartoon "fill" — no realistic gore. It's built for a laugh and a wince, not a horror show.

Free to play, with optional ads and purchases. Remove ads any time, and every hat is drawn by hand in code — no downloads, no bloat.

So go on. The giant's asleep. The blood is warm. Just one more sip.
