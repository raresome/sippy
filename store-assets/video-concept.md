# SIPPY — Store Video Concept (15–30s)

You shoot this yourself from real gameplay on a device. This is the shot list. The whole point of
SIPPY's marketing (spec §6) is the TikTok hook: **`vox_onemoresip` + slap + harp**. The store video
should feel like that hook.

## The single most impressive moment to lead with
**A frame-perfect CLUTCH escape** — the giant's hand rears back, the danger vignette pulses red, and
Sippy shoots out of frame 0.05s before the slap connects, "CLUTCH! ×1.5" popping on screen. That one
beat sells the entire risk-reward fantasy. Capture a real one; it's worth the retries.

## Shot list (≈22s)

| Time | Shot | On-screen caption (muted-friendly) |
|------|------|-----------------------------------|
| 0–3s | **HOOK.** Cold open on the sip: Sippy drinking, mL counter ticking up fast, giant's brow twitching. Squeaky "one more sip…" plays. | "how much do you dare?" |
| 3–7s | Meter climbs into the red, snore turns agitated, danger vignette pulses. Hold the tension. | "just… a little… more" |
| 7–11s | The WINDUP. Hand rears back. Freeze a half-beat. | "LET GO." |
| 11–14s | **The payoff split** — cut A: a clean CLUTCH escape + fanfare ("CLUTCH! ×1.5", combo counter). | "or don't." |
| 14–17s | cut B: a SPLAT. Slap SFX, screen shake, the ghost floats up (still wearing its hat) + harp. | "greed got him." |
| 17–20s | Quick montage: hat shop, a rainbow trail, a big banked-mL number on the game-over card. | "upgrade. dress up. try again." |
| 20–22s | **End card:** app icon + "SIPPY — one more sip." + "Download free". | — |

## Direction notes
- Shoot **portrait 9:16** for the Play listing and for cross-posting to TikTok/Shorts/Reels.
- Keep the focal subject **out of the center-bottom third** — Play overlays a play button there.
- Many viewers watch muted: the captions above must carry the story on their own.
- Cut on the beat. The slap and the harp are your edit points — let them land.
- Real gameplay only. No mockups. The comedy is in the actual splat.

## Music / audio
- The game's own SFX are the soundtrack: `vox_onemoresip`, the buzz, the slap, the harp, the clutch
  fanfare. Don't overlay licensed music that fights them.
- If you want a bed track or extra sting, the ElevenLabs MCP can generate SFX to match (spec §6).

## Alt 8-second cut (for ads / Shorts)
Hook (0–3s sip) → WINDUP freeze "LET GO" (3–5s) → CLUTCH escape + fanfare (5–7s) → icon end card
(7–8s). This is the single most reusable clip; make it first.
