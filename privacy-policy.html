Privacy Policy

Gen Art Studios ("we", "us") built SIPPY as a free game supported by ads and optional in-app purchases. This policy explains what data the app collects, why, and the choices you have. We keep it short because there isn't much to explain — SIPPY is a single-player arcade game with no accounts and no sign-up.

The short version

  • SIPPY has no login and asks for no personal information — no name, email, phone, or location.
  • Your game progress is stored only on your device and is deleted when you uninstall.
  • We show ads through Google AdMob, which uses your device's Advertising ID.
  • We measure how the game is played with Google Firebase Analytics (anonymous, aggregated).
  • Purchases are handled by Google Play Billing.
  • We do not sell your data, and everything is sent over encrypted connections.

Information we collect

We do not ask you for personal information, and SIPPY has no user accounts. The only data that leaves your device is collected automatically by the third-party services below.

1. Advertising data (Google AdMob)

To show ads, the Google Mobile Ads SDK accesses your device's Advertising ID (a resettable identifier) and limited device / ad-interaction information, used to serve and measure ads and, where you consent, to personalize them. In the EEA, UK, and Switzerland we present a consent choice on first launch; if you decline or consent is unavailable, ads are shown in a non-personalized form. AdMob data is processed by Google under Google's Privacy Policy.

2. Analytics data (Google Firebase Analytics)

We record anonymous, aggregated gameplay events — such as when a run ends, when you get splatted, how much you banked, and when a hat is equipped — so we can understand what's fun and fix what isn't. These events are tied to an app-instance identifier, not to your identity, and are not used to contact or track you across other apps.

3. Purchase data (Google Play Billing)

If you make an in-app purchase, Google Play processes the transaction and shares purchase status with the app so it can unlock the content. We never see or store your payment details. Purchases are non-consumable and can be restored on a new device through Google Play.

4. Data stored only on your device

Your best score, closest call, banked "blood", owned hats and trails, equipped cosmetics, upgrade levels, sound setting, and consent choice are saved locally. This data stays on your device, is not uploaded to us, and is removed when you uninstall.

What we do NOT collect

  • No name, email, phone number, or account.
  • No location data.
  • No contacts, photos, files, microphone, or camera access.
  • No health, financial, or biometric data.

Your choices

  • Ad personalization: In the EEA/UK/Switzerland, change your consent any time from Settings → Privacy options inside the game.
  • Reset your Advertising ID / opt out: Android Settings → Privacy → Ads.
  • Delete your data: Uninstalling SIPPY removes all locally stored game data. To request deletion of analytics data associated with your app instance, contact us. Data held by Google is retained per Google's own policies and Firebase defaults.

Children

SIPPY is directed to a general audience aged 13 and older and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data security

All data transmitted by the SDKs above is sent over encrypted (HTTPS/TLS) connections. On-device game data is stored within the app's private storage sandbox.

Changes to this policy

If we change how the app handles data, we will update this page and the "Last updated" date above, and — where required — surface the change in the app.
